A community of care and curiosity

We believe food created with care and enjoyed with good company is just as essential to health as any specific nutrient. Bread has been at the core of communities for millenia and its ability to support wellbeing is rooted in the way it brings people together. Curiosity and conviviality around the table is a key to growth, learning, and health.

Simple ingredients, patient practice

Our bread is made with four ingredients: flour, water, salt, and wild yeast—plus a whole lot of care and patience. We're always practicing new techniques and exploring ingredients to make an even tastier bread.

Local food creativity

We are committed to developing a supply chain that is sustainable, contributes to local economic development, and celebrates our unique local food identity through collaboration with local farmers and producers.
